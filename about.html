ABOUT ME

My name is Matthew Wilson and I am a Front-End Web Developer. I received a Bachelors Degree in Computer Graphics Technology with a Minor in Computer and Information Systems from Purdue University. I am focusing on Website Development and Design. I am the second son in an awesome family from Lawrenceburg, Indiana. From a young age I've been fascinated by what computers can do.

Web Design, UI Design, Mobile Development, and Web Development are among some of my interests.
